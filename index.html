About

Sited on a six-acre campus on the Channi Himmat hillock in Jammu — the winter capital of the strife-torn northern state of Jammu & Kashmir — Jodhamal Public School (JMPS, estb. 2005) has established a reputation as the state’s top-ranked primary-secondary in a short span of time. JMPS is ranked #1 in J&K and among the Top 100 schools nationally in the co-ed day schools category for consecutive 6 years. JMPS has been awarded a 6-Star Gold Rating by the UK-based World Education Foundation for providing a wholesome nurturing environment for children. Games and sports facilities include a half Olympic size swimming pool, football/cricket ground, basketball court, aerobics studio, handball, martial arts, skating rink facilities, athletics field, badminton court, and table tennis room. To fulfill its institutional objective of providing quality K-12 education on a world-class campus in Jammu, the JMPS top management has invested heavily in academic, co-curricular and sports facilities.
